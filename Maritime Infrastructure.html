Service Description

Our team provides electrical services within hazardous operating areas. We can work in any industry but principally add value to the defence and forecourt sectors, where we prevent fires, explosions and electrocutions as well as unwanted leakage of currents for those who are responsible for watertight compliance.

Our Services

We are committed in providing reliable generator supply solution to cater your power requirements and needs. With world class engineered diesel and gas generators from manufacturers such as Cummins, Perkins, Scania and Caterpillar, our offer of reliable and high-quality generators at very competitive prices makes us the perfect choice for your business

HV/LV Service (Substation, Overhead and Underground)

Our team offers an end-to-end service that will ensure your electrical infrastructure requirements fulfilled. Working alongside you while providing a unique range of services for designing, construction, commissioning, inspection and maintenance of distribution networks ranging from 415V to 33kV.

Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD)

HDD is a non-drilling method that is very protective to the environment and can be done anytime without taking large workspace. HDD able to avoid obstruction effectively. Our experience ensures client are able to utilize HDD to its full extent.
