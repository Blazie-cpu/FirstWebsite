About Us

MH Integrated Systems Sdn Bhd was establish in 25th October 2015 with a goal to supply, create and deploy a seamless integration between front and back-end system on all level of industries for our client. With experienced and talent personnel, we believe we can achieve that target and at the same time bring mutual trust and growth to all parties involved with mutual benefit in terms of profitability and knowledge.

  • Our Vision

We have a vision to accelerate the growth of our clients in this age of fast pace information and data, where everything is possible with the right technology.

We believe on providing clients the most cost effective way to implement new software and hardware into their system needs, without lowering the standard and quality of our products, and the same time educating our client the importance of having the best technology available and how it can help them grow their business

  • Our Mission

To provide an easy way for our client to integrate the bleeding edge in technolgy advancement into existing system, at the same time educating customers about the benefit gain by using the latest technology available.

We confident in our work that we can seamlessly integrate any existing system with the latest hardware and software with maximum compatibility with exisiting hardware.

All this with cost effectiveness that satisfies our client in terms of quality, high standard compared to competition and cost effectiveness.
